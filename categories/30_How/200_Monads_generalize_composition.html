Monads generalize composition

Example with lists:

f=\x->[x]        ⇒ f 1 = [1]    ⇒ (f.f) 1 = [[1]]
g=\x->[x+1]      ⇒ g 1 = [2]    ⇒ (g.g) 1 = ERROR [2]+1
h=\x->[x+1,x*10] ⇒ h 1 = [2,10] ⇒ (h.h) 1 = ERROR [2,10]+1

How to fix that? x >>= f = join (fmap f x) (join is ⊙)

f=\x->[x]        ⇒ f 1 = [1]    ⇒ f 1 >>= f = [1]
g=\x->[x+1]      ⇒ g 1 = [2]    ⇒ g 1 >>= g = [3]
h=\x->[x+1,x*10] ⇒ h 1 = [2,10] ⇒ h 1 >>= h = [3,20,11,100]
