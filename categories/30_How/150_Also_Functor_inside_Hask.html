Also Functor inside \(\Hask\)

length can be seen as a Functor from the category [a] to the cateogry Int:

  • \(\ob{\mathtt{[a]}}=\{∙\}\)
  • \(\hom{\mathtt{[a]}}=\mathtt{[a]}\)
  • \(∘=\mathtt{(++)}\)

⇒

  • \(\ob{\mathtt{Int}}=\{∙\}\)
  • \(\hom{\mathtt{Int}}=\mathtt{Int}\)
  • \(∘=\mathtt{(+)}\)
  • id: length [] = 0
  • comp: length (l ++ l') = (length l) + (length l')
