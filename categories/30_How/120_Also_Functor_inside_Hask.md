## Also Functor inside \\(\\Hask\\)

`length` can be seen as a Functor from the category `[a]` to `Int`.
More precisely:

- \\(\\ob{\\mathtt{[a]}}=\\{∙\\}\\)
- \\(\\hom{\\mathtt{[a]}}=\\mathtt{[a]}\\)
- \\(∘=\\mathtt{(++)}\\)

&

- \\(\\ob{\\mathtt{Int}}=\\{∙\\}\\)
- \\(\\hom{\\mathtt{Int}}=\\mathtt{Int}\\)
- \\(∘=\\mathtt{(+)}\\)
